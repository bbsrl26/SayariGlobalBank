Sayari Global Bank

Rising from the legacy of Planet Sayari Bank, the new Sayari Global Bank will be incorporated in Cyprus and positioned as a next‑generation financial institution for Africa, South America, and post‑Soviet markets.

Our Vision

Sayari Global Bank is designed as a crypto‑friendly, technology‑driven institution built on the advanced financial infrastructure developed by Siegfried Asset Management. From Cyprus, we will expand across emerging markets, offering modern banking, digital asset services, and innovative financial programs.

Core Services

• Tokenization of Real‑World Assets (RWA) • Multi‑asset tokenomics and proprietary digital currencies • Capital management for third parties • Monetization of banking instruments • Financial programs and structured funding • Small‑funding and development financing • Full integration with the Siegfried banking application

Transition from Planet Sayari Bank

Planet Sayari Bank remains operational during the transition phase. However, it will soon be replaced by the more prestigious and globally oriented Sayari Global Bank, reflecting our evolution into a modern, compliant, and internationally scalable institution.
